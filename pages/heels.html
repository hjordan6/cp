All things UNC

UNC has been a mystery this year. Big wins against teams like Gonzaga and Virginia Tech make them look like a force to be reckoned with come march, while struggles against Michigan on the raod, and even worse, Louisvile at home, leave college basketball fans wondering if they can make it past the first round.

Projected lottery pick, Freshman Nassir Little's play has been anything but consistent. While many accuse Roy Williams of limiting his playing time in order to prevent a one and done, his inconsitency and sloppieness make it hard to justify giving him a regular starting role.

Luke Maye remains the face of the team, as he should. In his senior year he continues to impress, not just with his skill as a basketball player, but in his ability to lead and inspire others. Whether or not he'll be a presence in the NBA remains to be seen, but he will go down as a UNC great without a doubt.
